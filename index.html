Tourist Attractions in Nigeria

Tourism in Nigeria centers largely on events, due to the country's ample amount of ethnic groups, but also includes rain forests, savannah, waterfalls, and other natural attractions. Tourists spent 2,6 billion USD in Nigeria in 2015, this dropped to 1.5 billion USD in 2017

Some Tourist Attractions

Nike Art Gallery

Nike Art Centre, Lagos, is one of Nigeria's hidden treasures. Located in Lekki, Lagos.

Guarara Waterfalls

One of the easiest waterfall to access in Nigeria. 3 Hours away from Abuja in Niger.

National Theatre

The National Theatre, Nigeria is the primary centre for the performing arts in Nigeria.

Kajuru Castle

Kajuru Castle is a luxury villa, built between the years 1981 and 1989, in southern Kaduna State
